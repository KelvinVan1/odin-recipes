Recipe for Japanese Curry

Image source: Tasty Kitchen

Description

Japanese Curry may look daunting at first but making this delicious Curry dish could not be as easy as I am about to show. All you will need is a bit of time and prep work and you have a dinner that will be able to last you a couple of days and can be spiced up each day with different dishes to keep things from getting boring.

Ingredients

  • Boxed Curry Mix (Any spice level you wish)
  • Water
  • Diced Onion
  • Minced Garlic
  • Peeled and cut Potatoes
  • Peeled and cut Carrots
  • Diced Beef
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Cooked Rice

Steps

  1. Start by adding oil and beef into a pot to brown but not fully cook
  2. Once beef browned take and place in bowl for later, add diced onion and oil to pot and cook until tender
  3. add garlic and allow to slighly brown
  4. Add water as according to boxed curry mix along with carrots and potatoes
  5. Once water comes to boil add boxed curry to water stirring until dissolved
  6. Add beef back into pot and allow everything to cook until potatoes and carrots tender
  7. Serve with rice and enjoy
