Description

Lasagna is a simple dish to follow which can be made in advance to feed many people at a party or a family at home. When following this basic recipe, this opens the opportunity to customize or edit the dish to your own liking.

Ingredients

  • Dry Lasagna Noodles
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Egg
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Any easily meltable cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles in a pot of salted boiling water
  2. Once tender drain water and leave on side for later use
  3. Brown some ground beef with some oil in another seperate pan
  4. Mix egg with ricotta Cheese in a bowl
  5. Begin by spreading some of the marinara sauce on the bottom of the baking dish
  6. Layer the bottom with the cooked lasagna noodles
  7. Add beef, ricotta cheese mix and regular cheese to layer
  8. Add more lasagna noodles ontop and repeat process
  9. Once to the top add some more regular cheese ontop and bake
  10. Once baked let rest to cool so lasagna holds shape
  11. Cut and serve!
